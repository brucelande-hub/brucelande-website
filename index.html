About Bruce

Singer. Guitarist. Entertainer.

Bruce Lande is a singer and guitar player with over six decades of experience entertaining audiences in Texas, New York and North Carolina. He focuses on crowd-pleasing songs that get everybody on their feet.

From his first solo act in San Angelo, Texas in 1970 while serving in the USAF, to the Syracuse club scene with his band Reflections Two, to hosting an international community of performers during the COVID pandemic — Bruce has spent a lifetime connecting people through music.

Today he performs at clubs, private parties, and community events across Eastern North Carolina from his home base in Holden Beach.
